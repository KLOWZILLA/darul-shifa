✧ Our Story

Healing through
the Sunnah

Darul-Shifa — House of Healing — was founded on a simple yet profound belief: that the prophetic traditions of medicine deserve a respected place in modern life. Hijama, or wet cupping therapy, has been practiced for over 5,000 years and was highly commended by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ as a means of maintaining health and treating illness.

"The best of your remedies is cupping." — Sahih al-Bukhari

Our practitioners combine traditional knowledge with modern hygiene standards and professional training — ensuring every session is safe, effective, and conducted with the utmost care and respect.
